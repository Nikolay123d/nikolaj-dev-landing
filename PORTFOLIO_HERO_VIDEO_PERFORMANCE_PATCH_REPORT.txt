Mobile / Facebook behavior:
- If userAgent includes FBAN, FBAV, FB_IAB, Instagram or Messenger, video is disabled and poster remains.
- If prefers-reduced-motion is enabled, video is disabled and poster remains.
- This prioritizes smooth loading over forcing autoplay in unstable in-app browsers.
